The problem

Importing into the EU
shouldn't be a guessing game.

01
📋
Surprise costs at the border
Duties, VAT, and handling fees appear only when your shipment arrives — blowing your margin calculations and causing cash flow chaos.
02
📊
HS codes are a nightmare
Finding the right Harmonized System code for your product requires expert knowledge. Get it wrong and you face penalties or pay more duty than needed.
03
🔄
FX rates change daily
Customs values must be declared in EUR. Manual currency conversions using stale rates introduce errors that compound across large shipments.
04
🤝
FTA rules are complex
The EU has 42+ free trade agreements. Knowing which apply to goods entering Luxembourg, and whether you qualify for preferential rates, takes hours of research.
